BallJumper

BallJumper is a 2D-Platformer game. The first game that I did. BallJumper have two levels, the first one is easier than the second one. In the game, the player tries to complete the level as fast as he can and collect the coins as much as possible. The player have movement mechanics and jump mechanics. I made all of in this project, but not graphics and game objects because we used ready-made assets in this project.
This project was a solo project.
